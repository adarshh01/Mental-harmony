Mental health made for everyone.

Mental illnesses affect 19% of the adult population, 46% of teenagers and 13% of children each year. People struggling with their mental health may be in your family, live next door, teach your children, work in the next cubicle or sit in the same church pew. However, only half of those affected receive treatment, often because of the stigma attached to mental health.
Millions of employees struggle with mental health challenges, leading to trouble with productivity, wellbeing, burnout, turnover, and more.

Employees need mental wellbeing support now more than ever.
